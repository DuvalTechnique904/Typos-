What is TYPOS?

With a wide variety of internet services available on the market, there is bound to be spam, malware, and bots attaching themselves to reputable sites in an attempt to sway the users’ perception of the product. User reviews give the buyer a look into the product, with poorly reviewed products staying unpurchased and well reviewed products becoming widespread. Online markets are fueled by reputation, and with competitors having an arsenal of tools that have the ability to stain a brand name, it is difficult for the honest opinions to shine. This is where Typos comes into play.
